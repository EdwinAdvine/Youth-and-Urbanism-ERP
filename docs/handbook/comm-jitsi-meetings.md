# Video Meetings with Jitsi

Urban Vibes Dynamics includes a fully self-hosted Jitsi Meet server. Every video meeting runs on your own infrastructure — no Zoom subscription, no Google Meet, no data leaving your environment. Both internal team members and external guests can join.

## Starting an Instant Meeting

For an unscheduled call right now:

1. Go to **Calendar** and click **Instant Meeting** (or use the quick-action button in the top navigation bar).
2. Urban Vibes Dynamics creates a new Jitsi room and opens it immediately in a browser tab.
3. Copy the **Join Link** from the meeting panel and share it with anyone you want to invite — via chat, email, or WhatsApp.

The meeting room stays open until all participants leave.

## Scheduling a Meeting in Advance

1. Create a **New Event** in Calendar (see the Calendar article for full steps).
2. In the **Location** field, click **Add Jitsi Video Link**.
3. Urban Vibes Dynamics generates a unique room URL and embeds it in the event.
4. When you add attendees, the invitation email they receive contains the Jitsi link automatically — they click it at the scheduled time to join.

External attendees (customers, vendors, contractors) do not need an Urban Vibes Dynamics account. They open the link in any modern browser and join directly.

## During the Meeting

Once inside the Jitsi room you have access to the standard meeting controls:

- **Mute/unmute** your microphone and toggle your camera on or off.
- **Screen sharing**: click the screen icon to share your entire screen, a specific application window, or a browser tab. Useful for presenting proposals, walking through a spreadsheet, or doing a live demo.
- **In-meeting chat**: send text messages to all participants without interrupting the speaker.
- **Raise hand**: signal that you want to speak.
- **Tile view / speaker view**: switch between seeing all participants at once or focusing on the active speaker.
- **Invite more people**: copy and share the room URL at any time during the meeting.

## Meeting Notes

When a scheduled Jitsi meeting ends, Urban Vibes Dynamics automatically creates a **Meeting Notes** entry in the Notes module linked to that calendar event. The note is pre-filled with the meeting title, date, attendees, and a blank agenda/minutes template. Any team member who attended can open the note and fill in action points, decisions made, and follow-up tasks while details are still fresh.

## Recording

If recording is enabled by your Super Admin (requires sufficient server storage), a **Record** button appears in the meeting toolbar. Recordings are saved to the Drive module under a **Meeting Recordings** folder. Participants receive a link to the recording once it has finished processing.

## Who Gets the Jitsi Link

- **Urban Vibes Dynamics users** who are added as attendees see the Jitsi link directly on the event in their Calendar.
- **External guests** receive the link in their invitation email.
- Anyone with the room URL can join — so treat the link like a meeting room key and only share it with intended participants.
