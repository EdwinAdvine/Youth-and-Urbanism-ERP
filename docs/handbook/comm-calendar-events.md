# Calendar: Creating Meetings & Events

The Urban Vibes Dynamics Calendar is your central scheduling hub. You can plan internal meetings, customer calls, field visits, and recurring events — all linked to CRM activities and project tasks so nothing falls through the cracks.

## Creating a New Event

1. Open the **Calendar** module from the left sidebar.
2. Click **New Event** (top-right) or click directly on a date/time slot in the calendar grid.
3. Fill in the event details:
   - **Title**: a short, descriptive name (e.g., "Q1 Sales Review" or "Client Call — Nairobi Breweries")
   - **Date & Time**: start and end time. Toggle **All Day** for events without a specific time.
   - **Location**: type a physical address, meeting room, or click **Add Jitsi Video Link** to auto-generate a Jitsi meeting URL (see below).
4. Click **Save** to create the event.

## Adding Attendees

In the **Attendees** field, search for Urban Vibes Dynamics users by name or type any external email address. Each attendee receives an email invitation with the event details and an Accept/Decline link. Their RSVP status appears on the event card in your calendar.

## Jitsi Video Links

When you click **Add Jitsi Video Link**, Urban Vibes Dynamics generates a unique meeting room URL on your organisation's self-hosted Jitsi server — no Zoom or Google Meet account required. The link is included automatically in the invitation email sent to all attendees. External attendees (outside Urban Vibes Dynamics) can join by clicking the link in their email; they do not need an Urban Vibes Dynamics account.

## Recurring Events

For events that repeat, enable the **Recurrence** toggle and select a pattern:

- **Daily** — repeats every day (or every N days)
- **Weekly** — repeats on selected days of the week (e.g., every Monday and Wednesday)
- **Monthly** — repeats on the same date each month or on a relative day (e.g., the first Friday of every month)
- **Custom end date** — stop the recurrence after a specific date or after a set number of occurrences

When editing a recurring event, you can choose to update **only this occurrence** or **all future occurrences**.

## Colour-Coding by Category

Assign a colour category to each event to make your calendar easier to scan at a glance. For example: red for customer meetings, blue for internal reviews, green for deadlines. Categories are configured in **Calendar → Settings → Categories**.

## Reminders

Set one or more reminders per event — for example, 15 minutes before and 1 day before. Reminders are delivered as in-app notifications and optionally by email.

## CRM and Project Integration

- **CRM Activities**: when you create an event from a CRM deal or contact record, the event appears in the CRM activity timeline automatically. Conversely, from Calendar you can click **Link to CRM** on any event to associate it with a deal or contact.
- **Project Tasks**: events created from a Project task show up in both the Calendar and the task detail view. Completing the task marks the calendar event as done.

This cross-module linking means your sales team's customer meetings, your project manager's deadlines, and your team's internal stand-ups all live in one shared calendar — with context from wherever they originated.
