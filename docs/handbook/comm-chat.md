# Team Chat

Urban Vibes Dynamics's built-in Chat module replaces the need for a separate messaging app. All conversations happen inside the platform, alongside your CRM, projects, and finance data — making it easy to share context without switching tools.

## Channels

Channels are group conversations organised by topic, team, or project.

**Creating a channel:**

1. In the Chat sidebar, click the **+** next to **Channels**.
2. Enter a channel name (e.g., `#sales-team`, `#project-phoenix`, `#general`).
3. Choose **Public** (any team member can join) or **Private** (invite-only).
4. Add initial members and click **Create Channel**.

Public channels are discoverable — team members can browse and join them from **Chat → Browse Channels**. Private channels are hidden from non-members.

## Direct Messages

To send a private message to a specific colleague:

1. Click the **+** next to **Direct Messages** in the sidebar.
2. Search for the person by name and click their name.
3. The DM thread opens. Type and press Enter to send.

You can also start a group DM with multiple people at once.

## @Mentions

Type `@username` in any message to notify a specific person. They receive an in-app notification and the message is highlighted in their **Mentions** view. Use `@channel` to notify everyone in the current channel (use sparingly).

## Emoji Reactions

Hover over any message and click the **emoji** icon to add a reaction. Reactions are a lightweight way to acknowledge a message without cluttering the thread with replies like "noted" or "thanks".

## Thread Replies

To reply to a specific message without disrupting the main channel flow:

1. Hover over the message and click **Reply in Thread**.
2. A thread panel opens on the right. Your replies are nested under the original message.

Thread participants receive notifications for each reply. Threads keep focused discussions organised, especially in busy channels.

## File Attachments

Click the **paperclip** icon to attach a file to any message. Files are stored in Urban Vibes Dynamics Drive automatically — so they are version-controlled, searchable, and accessible from the Drive module later. Attachments appear as inline previews in the chat.

## Pinned Messages

To pin an important message (e.g., a channel description, a key decision, or a shared document link):

1. Hover over the message and click **... → Pin to Channel**.
2. Pinned messages are accessible from the **Pins** icon at the top of the channel — useful for keeping team agreements or key links easily findable.

## Search

The search bar (top of the Chat module) supports full-text search across all channels and DMs you have access to. Filter results by channel, user, or date range.

## Presence Status

Your status is shown as a coloured dot next to your avatar:

- **Green** — Online and active
- **Yellow** — Away (no activity for a set period)
- **Red** — Do Not Disturb (notifications silenced)
- **Grey** — Offline

Set your own status manually by clicking your avatar (top-right) and selecting a status. You can also set a custom status message (e.g., "In meetings until 3pm" or "Out of office — back Monday").
