# CRM Activities & Follow-Ups

Activities are the record of every interaction with a lead or deal — calls, emails, meetings, demos, and notes. Keeping activities logged gives your team a complete timeline of each relationship and ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

---

## 1. Activity Types

Urban Vibes Dynamics supports the following activity types:

| Type | Use for |
|---|---|
| Call | Phone or voice call with a prospect or client |
| Email | Outbound email (logged manually or auto-synced from Mail module) |
| Meeting | In-person or virtual meeting |
| Note | Internal observation or reminder (not sent to the contact) |
| Task | Action item with a due date (appears in your task list) |
| Demo | Product demonstration session |

---

## 2. Logging an Activity from a Lead or Deal

1. Open the lead or deal
2. Go to the **Timeline** tab
3. Click **Log Activity**
4. Select the activity type and complete the form (see section 3)
5. Click **Save**

The activity appears immediately on the timeline and is visible to anyone with access to the record.

---

## 3. Activity Form Fields

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Type | Call, Email, Meeting, Note, Task, Demo |
| Date & Time | When the activity occurred (or is scheduled) |
| Subject | Brief description, e.g., "Discovery call" |
| Description | Full notes — what was discussed, what was agreed |
| Linked Contact | Which contact at the company this activity relates to |
| Outcome | Result: Positive, Neutral, Negative, No Answer |
| Next Action | What happens next (free text) |
| Due Date | For Tasks — when the follow-up is due |

---

## 4. Scheduled Activities (Reminders)

Setting a **Due Date** on any activity type creates a scheduled reminder:

- The activity appears in your **My Tasks** list (top bar) with a countdown
- It also appears in the **Calendar** module as a task block on the due date
- You receive an in-app notification when the due date arrives
- Once completed, mark it **Done** — it moves from active tasks to the timeline history

Scheduled activities without a due date remain open indefinitely.

> **Tip:** Log every call immediately after hanging up, while it's fresh. Even a 2-line note beats nothing — it protects you if a deal's ownership is ever disputed.

---

## 5. Activity Timeline

Every lead and deal has a **Timeline** tab showing all interactions in chronological order:

- Stage changes (with timestamp and who moved it)
- Logged activities (calls, emails, notes)
- Emails sent and received (if Mail module is linked)
- System events (deal created, owner changed)
- Comments from team members

The timeline is read-only — you cannot delete entries, only add new ones. This preserves an audit trail.

---

## 6. Bulk Activity Logging

To log the same activity against multiple contacts at once:

1. Go to **CRM → Leads** or **CRM → Contacts**
2. Filter to the target group (e.g., all Qualified leads assigned to you)
3. Select records using the checkboxes
4. Click **Log Activity** in the bulk action toolbar
5. Complete the activity form — it will be applied to all selected records

Useful for follow-up campaigns: "Send follow-up email to all prospects from last month's webinar."

---

## 7. Overdue Activities

Navigate to **CRM → Activities** for a dedicated activities view.

Filter options:

| Filter | Shows |
|---|---|
| Overdue | Due date has passed, not marked done |
| Due Today | Due today |
| Upcoming | Due in the next 7 days |
| All Open | Any activity not yet marked done |
| Completed | Activities marked done |

The overdue count also appears as a badge on the CRM navigation item — a quick prompt to follow up.

---

## 8. Team Activity View (Manager)

Managers and users with CRM Admin access can see all team activities:

1. **CRM → Activities → Team View**
2. Filter by: Rep, Activity Type, Date Range, Lead/Deal Stage

Use this view to:
- Spot reps who are not logging activities (potential coaching opportunity)
- Identify which reps are most active on specific deal types
- Review activity leading up to a deal that was lost

---

## 9. Activity Reports

Go to **CRM → Reports → Activities**.

| Report | Description |
|---|---|
| Calls per rep | Volume of logged calls by rep and date range |
| Meetings per week | Meeting count trend over time |
| Activity to stage change | % of activities that are followed by a stage advancement within N days |
| Activity distribution | Breakdown of activity types across the team |
| Follow-up compliance | % of deals with at least one activity in the last 7/14/30 days |

Export any report to CSV using the **Export** button.
